About TISA !!

Every school is recognized by how well it prepares and inspires each child along their journey towards success and accomplishments.


At TISA, we create a unique environment and space for children to grow holistically, with an atmosphere where each child feels safe, valued and cared. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds to achieve high academic proficiency and develop a holistic personality. Responsible Citizen point can be brought up later in connection with our commitment to the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. At TISA, we believe 'To Initiate' a learning revolution, 'To Inspire' the future generation and 'To innovate' keys to success. With these three core elements of our learning practice; TISA has emerged as the best school in Agra. TISA has got the recognition as the best CBSE School in Agra because of its effective teaching methods, which allows students to understand the concepts easily. We try to make learning as easy as possible with the help of interactive studies. Without any bias and prejudice, we let our students to discover their own capabilities and to excel in them.

Why we are different from Others

What makes The International School Agra different from others is our unwavering dedication to creating a safe and secure learning environment so that our students can grow into compassionate, self-assured, and responsible global citizens. We believe in the ability of the young mind to alter the world since we are one of the top schools in Agra. We try to invest in our students the virtues of empathy, accountability, modesty, and the drive to believe in their ambitions with this belief and faith.

TISA, one of the best CBSE schools in Agra, develops students' abilities in areas like arts, academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. We offer a space where students' minds can explore who they are and make decisions about the future. TISA's dedication to upholding the highest standards in teaching and learning is demonstrated by the outstanding results of our students in the board exams. Our devoted team of teachers supports students through their problems and helps each one fulfill their potential as one of the top CBSE schools in Agra.

Students at TISA acquire the ability to tolerate, encourage, and accept diversity in abilities and opinions. They will transcend the constrained notions of success and performance thanks to these values. Additionally, the school has a history of teacher and student exchanges to promote understanding of various viewpoints. We are a school community that is committed to this collaborative relationship between students, teachers, and parents that operates with consensus, openness, and responsibility. We anticipate receiving their ongoing assistance as TISA, one of the top schools in Agra expands and develops as a center of excellence.
