Admission Process &Fee Structure

At TISA, we have a quick 8 step admission process, starting from inquiry to a successful admission of the student. And we are constantly trying to make this process ever simpler everyday! Post admission inquiry, you are assigned an admission counsellor who helps determine the best course of action for your child moving further. The counsellor also helps you fill out the Admission Form. Step 4, in the process is your child sitting for an Entrance Test of their choice (see the table below for more details.) Post a successful clearance of the Entrance Test a brief meeting is set for the family with our Principal, so that all channels of communication always remain open and you get to experience first hand the TISA mission & vision. Further down the line, the Official Form and Document Submission takes place, followed by Fee Payment (you can find the fee structure, here .) You are provided a Fee Receipt and Admission Confirmation by our team, along with the details of the Session, thus marking a successful beginning for the student!

TISA Fee Structure

Please find below terms and conditions surrounding TISA Agra Fee Structure for the Academic Session 2024-2025, followed by a tabular representation of the same :

  • The cost of prospectus and application form is Rs700/-
  • School will provide the specifications and list of uniforms, textbooks, notebooks and shoes which can be purchased from any shop in the city.
  • Transport facility is optional and will be charged based on location of pick-up.
  • No component of the fee is refundable, in part or full, under any circumstances unless otherwise specifically mentioned.
  • Family concession where two or more students of same parents are enrolled, will be provided only for the younger sibling. The rebate permissible is 10% on Composite fees.
  • This rebate cannot be combined with any other scholarship / rebate/ waiver of fees
  • The rebate permissible is 50% on Admission Fees only, in the case of a single girl child.
  • The TISA fee structure is governed by its rules, policies, terms and conditions and is subject to amendment from time to time by the Management at its sole discretion.
  • Fees can be paid by cash, Online and demand draft only. Please mention Student Name, App. No Grade and all correspondence.
